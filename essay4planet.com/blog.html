Labor Essay on Indian Manufacturing

26 Dec 2017

The increasing use of workers hired on temporary contracts has been one of the most striking features of Indian labor markets. The proportion of temporary contract laborers has risen from 12-24% in the span of 20 years, and in some Indian states, these workers comprised by more than half of the manufacturing companies. In India, the contract labor act has been regulating the employment of workers on contact since 1970.
